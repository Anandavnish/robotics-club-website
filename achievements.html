Research Publications

Decentralized Coordination in Multi-Robot Systems

Published: IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters, 2025

Authors: R. Sharma, P. Singh, Dr. A. Kumar

Novel algorithms for efficient task allocation and coordination in swarm robotics applications, with applications in search and rescue operations.

Deep Learning for Robotic Grasping

Published: International Conference on Robotics (ICRA), 2024

Authors: K. Menon, D. Iyer, Dr. A. Kumar

A deep learning approach for robotic grasping of unknown objects using RGB-D sensors and convolutional neural networks.

Autonomous Navigation in Dynamic Environments

Published: Journal of Field Robotics, 2024

Authors: A. Patel, V. Joshi, Dr. A. Kumar

Advanced SLAM algorithms for mobile robot navigation in crowded and dynamic indoor environments with real-time obstacle avoidance.

Human-Robot Collaboration Framework

Published: ACM/IEEE International Conference on HRI, 2023

Authors: R. Gupta, M. Nair, Dr. A. Kumar

A framework for safe and efficient human-robot collaboration in industrial settings using gesture recognition and predictive motion planning.
