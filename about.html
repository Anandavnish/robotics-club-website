About Us

Pioneering Robotics Innovation Since 2010

Our Story

The Robotics Club at NIT Patna was established in 2010 by a group of passionate engineering students who envisioned creating a platform for robotics enthusiasts to learn, innovate, and excel. What started as a small group of 10 members has now grown into one of the most active and successful technical clubs in the institution.

Over the years, we have participated in numerous national and international competitions, won prestigious awards, and contributed significantly to the field of robotics through our research and projects. Our club has become a breeding ground for innovation, where students from various disciplines come together to push the boundaries of what's possible with robotics technology.

Today, we continue to inspire the next generation of roboticists, providing them with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities to transform their ideas into reality.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of innovation and excellence in robotics by providing students with hands-on experience, cutting-edge resources, and opportunities to collaborate on groundbreaking projects that address real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To become a leading center for robotics education and research in India, producing world-class engineers and innovators who will shape the future of autonomous systems and intelligent machines.

Our Objectives

Promote practical learning through workshops and projects, encourage participation in national and international competitions, facilitate research in emerging robotics technologies, and build a strong community of robotics enthusiasts.

What We Do

Competitions

We regularly participate in prestigious robotics competitions including ABU Robocon, TechFest IIT Bombay, and Smart India Hackathon. Our teams have consistently achieved top rankings and brought laurels to the institution.

Workshops & Training

We conduct regular workshops on Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ROS, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and other cutting-edge technologies. These sessions are designed to provide hands-on experience and practical knowledge.

Research & Development

Our members engage in cutting-edge research in areas like autonomous navigation, swarm robotics, human-robot interaction, and AI-powered robotic systems. We collaborate with faculty and industry partners on innovative projects.

Community Building

We organize tech talks, guest lectures from industry experts, and networking events to build a strong robotics community. Our alumni network spans across leading tech companies and research institutions worldwide.

Our Facilities

We are equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and advanced equipment to support our members' learning and project development.

Electronics Lab

Fully equipped with oscilloscopes, function generators, power supplies, soldering stations, and a wide range of electronic components for circuit design and testing.

Mechanical Workshop

3D printers, CNC machines, laser cutters, and traditional machining tools for fabricating robot chassis, mechanical components, and custom parts.

AI & Computing Lab

High-performance workstations with GPU support for running AI/ML algorithms, computer vision applications, and robot simulations using ROS and Gazebo.
