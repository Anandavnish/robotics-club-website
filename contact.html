Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Robotics Club?

Any student from NIT Patna can join the Robotics Club. We conduct recruitment drives at the beginning of each semester. Fill out the contact form above with "Join the Club" as the purpose, and we'll send you details about the next recruitment process. You can also attend our Saturday meetings to learn more about the club.

Do I need prior robotics experience to join?

No prior experience is required! We welcome students from all years and branches. We provide comprehensive training through workshops and mentorship programs. Whether you're interested in hardware, software, AI, or design, there's a place for you in our club.

How often are workshops conducted?

We conduct workshops regularly throughout the semester, typically 2-3 per month. Topics range from basic Arduino programming to advanced topics like ROS, machine learning, and computer vision. Workshop schedules are announced on our social media channels and during Saturday meetings.

Can I propose my own project idea?

Absolutely! We encourage members to propose innovative project ideas. You can present your idea during our Saturday meetings or submit it through the contact form. If approved, you'll receive mentorship, resources, and team support to bring your idea to life.

How can external organizations collaborate with the club?

We welcome collaborations with industry partners, research institutions, and other organizations. Please contact us via email at [email protected] with "Collaboration Proposal" in the subject line, or fill out the contact form above. We're open to sponsorships, joint projects, guest lectures, and internship opportunities.
